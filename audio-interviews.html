AUDIO BROADCASTING:

MAKE SOUND INVESTMENT
Keep the market informed with our audio broadcasting solution.

Boardroom.Media offer a complete range of corporate digital streaming solutions to help you communicate effectively to larger audiences. Why not enhance your corporate communications and increase transparency in your business with a professional audio interview.

Our simple, cost-effective broadcasting solutions will enhance your market communications and give you widespread exposure to the business community. These short-form interviews are ideal for online audiences and can be turned around quickly from recording to release.

All audio interviews are done over the phone and no additional hardware or software is required, meaning you can record from your desk, on-location or overseas.

Interviews will be released through the Boardroom.Media network to maximise exposure, and embed codes will be supplied so you can promote your interview on your own website.

Key benefits of audio interviews:
Control your message

Interviews can be fully scripted on request, and questions will be provided to you in advance. All recordings are sent to you for review, giving you the opportunity to make changes, or re-record if you’re not happy with the first take.

Access to global audiences

Your physical event can now have a global reach. Audiences in remote locations and different timezones can listen to your interview or company presentation at a time that is convenient for them.

Mobile compatible

Our platform was built with a mobile first approach. and is compatible with all of the major operating systems and web browsers. can be viewed on a mobile, tablet, laptop or desktop.

Expand your digital footprint.

A robust digital presence is key for businesses to increase visibility and create sustainable growth. Leverage the power of digital, social, web and multimedia content to create brand awareness and raise your profile.

Fast turn-around

After your interview has been recorded our production team will edit the audio file and upload it to our platform. A link to preview your interview will be sent to you for review within one hour of recording it.

Record from any location

Interviews can be conducted in person, or over the phone, no additionl hardware or software is required so you can record your message from your desk, on-location, or overseas
